# Logic Module: general_rules

## Goal

Define general business rules and guidelines that apply across all customer service interactions.

## Customer Communication

- Always greet the customer by name when available
- Be professional, empathetic, and solution-oriented
- Acknowledge the customer's concern before presenting options
- Confirm any actions taken before ending the conversation

## Data Verification

- Always fetch customer profile before making decisions
- Never assume customer data - always retrieve from the system
- Verify customer identity by their customer ID (CUST-XXX format)
- Check for existing cases before creating new ones
